## Quick Reference

### IP Protection Strategy
- **Trademarks**: Primary brand protection
- **Trade Secrets**: Core technology protection  
- **Patents**: Not pursued (practical considerations)
- **Copyright**: Automatic for original code and content

### Regulatory Jurisdictions
- **UAE**: Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) - primary
- **Egypt**: Law 151/2020 - secondary market
- **International**: GDPR-ready for potential EU expansion
- **Education**: UAE MoE, Egypt MoE standards

### SharePoint IP Governance Repository

**Location**: YHT Corporate SharePoint - PMO Records

**Key Documents** (v1.0):
- 11 Strategy & Planning Documents (D-IP-001 to D-IP-011)
- 4 Policies (PL-IP-001 to PL-IP-004)
- 8 Forms/Registers (F-IP-001 to F-IP-008)
- 2 Agreement Templates (T-IP-001, T-IP-002)
- Guidelines and Processes

### Compliance Standards
- **Data Privacy**: UAE PDPL, Egypt Law 151/2020, GDPR-aligned
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 Level AA
- **Localization**: Arabic-first, RTL, bilingual (Arabic/English)
- **Security**: Encryption (AES-256), TLS 1.3, RBAC, audit logging
