# Contact Directory

## Overview

Contact information for the Kinana development team is managed through Ibtikar Edu Tech Solutions corporate Office 365 directory. All team members are automatically included in the company Global Address List (GAL).

---

## Access Points

### Microsoft Outlook

**Primary contact directory interface**

Access team contacts:

1. Open Outlook (desktop or web)
2. Click "People" or address book icon
3. Search for team member by name
4. View contact details, organization chart, and availability

**Available Information**:

- Full name and title
- Email address
- Phone number(s)
- Office location
- Reporting structure
- Teams presence status

### Microsoft Teams

**Real-time contact access**

Access team contacts:

1. Open Microsoft Teams
2. Use search bar to find team member
3. View profile with contact details
4. Start chat or call directly
5. Check real-time availability status

**Features**:

- Instant messaging
- Video/audio calls
- Presence indicators (Available, Busy, Away, Do Not Disturb)
- Organization chart view

## Role-Based Contact Points

### Product & Business Inquiries

**Contact**: Product Manager  
**Via**: Outlook email or Teams message  
**Response Time**: Same business day

### Technical & Architecture Questions

**Contact**: Solution Architect  
**Via**: Teams message (for quick questions), email (for detailed discussions)  
**Response Time**: Same business day

### Development & Implementation

**Contact**: Senior Developer  
**Via**: Teams message or daily standup  
**Response Time**: Within working hours

### Testing & Sprint Coordination

**Contact**: QA / Project Manager  
**Via**: Teams message or GitHub Issue  
**Response Time**: Same business day

### Design & UX Questions

**Contact**: UI/UX Designer(s)  
**Via**: Teams message  
**Response Time**: Same business day

### Executive & Strategic Matters

**Contact**: CEO  
**Via**: Product Manager (primary), or direct email for urgent matters  
**Response Time**: As scheduled or within 24-48 hours

## Emergency Contact Protocol

### Production Issues (When Live)

1. **Contact**: Solution Architect + Senior Developer
2. **Method**: Teams message + phone call
3. **Escalation**: Product Manager if not resolved within 30 minutes
4. **Documentation**: Incident logged in GitHub Issue

### Security Incidents

1. **Contact**: Solution Architect immediately
2. **CC**: Product Manager and CEO
3. **Method**: Phone call + secure email
4. **Follow-up**: Formal incident report

### Business Critical Matters

1. **Contact**: Product Manager
2. **Escalation**: CEO if needed
3. **Method**: Phone call + email confirmation
